Meet the Team

Many hands make light work…

…but the right hands do the best work!

Ignition - Integrated Ideas People is not like the other generic direct marketing suppliers. Rather than simply cleaning data or processing direct mail campaigns, we work closely with clients to enable you to maximise the return from your investment in data management and customer communications.

We achieve this by working in partnership with you, in order to understand and satisfy your needs. We continually strive to act, not like just another supplier but like an extension of your own organisation.

Our overall objective is to assist all of our clients in developing strong, long-lasting and meaningful relationships with their target audiences. Relationships that will build loyalty, increase revenue and strengthen your business.

The key to achieving this synergistic, rather than transactional, approach to client service has been our investment in people. We pride ourselves on having a strong, highly-experienced client services team, with in-depth knowledge and a passion for exceeding client expectations.
